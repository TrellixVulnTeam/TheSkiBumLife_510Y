The Ski Bum Life

A guide to living the good life

Who am I and what's this site about?

My name is Jake Meyers, and I love to ski. There's nothing like waking up early, checking the snow report and uncontrolably smiling, knowing it's about to be a great day. You proceed to make a big breakfast, pocket some beers and snacks for later, and head to catch the bus. Get to the mountain with 10 minutes to spare. Grab a coffee and meet up with some friends giving a round of fist bumps as you guys discuss recent snow and where on the mountain to head first. The base is empty as it's 0 degrees and a Tuesday. Weekend warriors are stuck in their cubicle and it's too cold and early for hungover vacationers.

Before moving to a ski town, I'd always try to find articles about the life that I was missing out on. Fortunatly, there wasn't much out there, so instead of living vicariously through a blog post, I had to move. I'm hoping this website helps scratch an itch, but also makes you consider changing your lifestyle.
